应急监测水中N-甲基哌嗪的气相色谱法
张莘民　 程滢　 徐朝　 
摘　要：采用气相色谱直接进样法测定了水中N-甲基哌嗪，在3min内完成整个分析过程。直接进水样经毛细管柱分离水中的N-甲基哌嗪，用GC/FID测定，以保留时间定性，峰高定量。在浓度范围为8.44～80.32mg/L时，相对标准偏差为1.8%～2.9%，标准偏差为0.19～1.5mg/L。样品加标回收率为81%。标准曲线的相关系数为0.9992。检出限为0.08mg/L。
关键词：应急监测；　气相色谱；　直接进样；　N-甲基哌嗪；　水

Emergency Monitoring of N-methyl Piperazine in the Water by GC
ZHANG Xin-min（Taizhou Municipal Environmental Monitoring Station,　Taizhou　225300，　China）　
CHENG Ying（Taizhou Municipal Environmental Monitoring Station,　Taizhou　225300，　China）　
XU Chao（Taizhou Municipal Environmental Monitoring Station,　Taizhou　225300，　China）　
Abstract：Determination of N-methyl piperazine in the water by direct injection gas chromatography (GC).The method completed the whole analytic process in 3 min.The N-methyl piperazine has been determinated by capollary column GC/FID with direct injection.The sample is qualitatively determined by retention time and quantitatively determined by peak-height extend standard method.When the average concentration range is 8.44～80.32 mg/L,the relative deviation is 1.8%～2.9%,and the standard deviation is 0.19～1.50 mg/L.The recovery rate of sample is 81%,the correlation coefficient is 0.9992,and the determination limit is 0.08 mg/L.
Keywords：emergency monitoring;　gas chromatography;　N-methyl piperazine;　water
